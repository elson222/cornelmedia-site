About Evanex Pharmacy

Dedicated to the health and wellness of our community.

Our Story

Evanex Pharmacy is a comprehensive healthcare facility located in Agona Nkwanta. We have grown from a community pharmacy into a full-service health hub, now offering Laboratory and Ultrasound Scan services under one roof.

We are dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality medical services. Whether you need prescription medication, a diagnostic blood test, or an ultrasound scan, our qualified professionals are here to ensure you receive the best care possible.

Our Location & Community

We are conveniently located opposite the Ahantaman Rural Bank in Agona Nkwanta. Our central position makes it easy for residents of the Ahanta West district to access our pharmacy, laboratory, and scan center services daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

Our Mission

To provide professional pharmaceutical care, reliable diagnostics, and accessible medication to improve the health and well-being of our community.

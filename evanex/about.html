About Evanex Pharmacy

Dedicated to the health and wellness of our community.

Our Story

Evanex Pharmacy is a premier healthcare facility located in Agona Nkwanta in the Ahanta West Municipal District. We have grown to become a trusted name for comprehensive medical services.

We understand that a pharmacy is more than just a place to buy medicine—it is a sanctuary for health advice, a reliable source for wellness products, and a partner in your journey to better health. Our team of qualified professionals ensures that every patient receives personalized care and accurate information regarding their prescriptions.

Our Location & Community

We are conveniently located opposite the Ahantaman Rural Bank in Agona Nkwanta. Our central position makes it easy for residents of the Ahanta West district to access our pharmacy, laboratory, and scan center services daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

Our Mission

  • To provide genuine, high-quality pharmaceutical products.
  • To offer expert health counseling and medication therapy management.
  • To contribute to the overall well-being of the Western Region.

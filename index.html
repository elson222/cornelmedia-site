The Founder

Cornelius Quayson

Cornelius Quayson is a Ghanaian creative professional and founder of Cornel Media Productions, a full-service creative and digital agency based in East Legon, Accra. With a background in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Mines and Technology, he brings a rare combination of technical precision and cinematic storytelling to every project.

From corporate documentaries for multinational mining companies to drone coverage for engineering firms and social media management for foundations and brands, Cornel Media Productions delivers world-class creative output from Ghana for clients across Africa and beyond.

Common Questions

FAQs

What does Cornel Media Productions do?

Cornel Media Productions is a full-service creative and digital agency providing premium photography, cinematic videography, drone services, social media management, website development, and live streaming services.

Where is Cornel Media Productions based?

We are based in East Legon, Accra, and Tarkwa in the Western Region of Ghana. We travel extensively across Ghana and West Africa for client projects.

Who are your clients?

We work with multinational corporations, engineering firms, healthcare institutions, NGOs, fashion brands, and private individuals. Notable clients include Gold Fields Ghana, Caldor Africa Ltd, Tarkwa Fashion Week, and Evanex Pharmacy.

Laboratory Test Directory

Hematology & Parasitology

30 - 45 Minutes

Included Parameters: Full Blood Count (FBC), Malaria Screen (MPS), Hemoglobin Level, Blood Grouping & Rh typing.

Preparation: No special preparation required.

Serology & Immunology

45 - 60 Minutes

Included Parameters: Typhoid Widal Reaction, Hepatitis B Surface Antigen (HBsAg), Hepatitis C, HIV screening, Syphilis (VDRL).

Preparation: No special preparation required.

Clinical Biochemistry

1 - 2 Hours

Included Parameters: Fasting Blood Glucose (FBG), Random Blood Glucose (RBG), Renal Function Panel, Liver Enzyme Tests.

Preparation: Fasting blood tests require 8-12 hours of water-only fast prior to sample draw.

Urinalysis & Clinical Microscopy

30 Minutes

Included Parameters: Urine Routine examination, Urine Chemistry, Stool routine analysis, stool occult blood screen.

Preparation: Sample containers are provided at the laboratory. Clean catch midstream urine sample is required.

Fasting Guidelines Warning

If your physician has requested a Fasting Blood Glucose (FBG) or lipid panel test, please do not eat or drink anything (except plain water) for 8 to 12 hours before visiting our lab. If you are taking regular diabetic or blood pressure medicines, consult your doctor for instructions on whether to take them before the blood draw.

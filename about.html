Cornelius Quayson

Founder & Creative Director

Cornel Media Productions is a premium media production brand founded by Cornelius Quayson, a Creative Director and BSc Computer Science and Engineering holder.

With a strong background in AI, robotics, and tech startups, Cornelius brings a unique technical precision to the art of visual storytelling. His mission is to deliver cinematic visuals that not only look beautiful but also tell a compelling story.

Driven by values of quality, professionalism, and continuous improvement, Cornel Media Productions aims to be the leading media brand in Ghana, offering high-end services for weddings, corporate events, and personal brands.

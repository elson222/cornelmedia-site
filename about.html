Our Story

Based in East Legon, Accra and Tarkwa, Western Region, Cornel Media Productions is a full-service media powerhouse specializing in premium videography, photography, drone piloting, and multi-cam live streaming.

We approach every project with a cinematic eye. Whether it's an intimate elopement, a high-octane music video, or a large-scale corporate broadcast, our team utilizes industry-standard 4K cameras and advanced lighting techniques to ensure your vision is captured flawlessly.

Like the world's top streaming platforms, we believe in delivering content that is immersive, crystal clear, and emotionally resonant. From social media content creation to full broadcast productions, we cover it all.

"Visuals are good. Stories are better. We believe in delivering content that is immersive, crystal clear, and emotionally resonant. Let us tell yours."

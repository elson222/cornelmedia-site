Common Questions

FAQs

Do you travel outside Accra?

Yes! We're based in both Accra and Tarkwa (Western Region) and regularly travel throughout Ghana. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our base cities.

How long before I receive my photos/videos?

Edited photos are typically delivered within 5–7 business days. Videos take 1–3 weeks depending on length and complexity. Rush delivery is available at an additional fee.

What is your booking process?

Contact us via WhatsApp or the contact form. We'll discuss your project, send a quote, and require a 50% deposit to secure your date. The balance is due on the day of the event.

Can I get a custom package?

Absolutely. Our Premium tier is fully customizable. Contact us with your brief and we'll build a package tailored to your exact needs and budget.

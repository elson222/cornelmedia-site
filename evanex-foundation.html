Who We Are

A Grassroots Force
for Change in Ghana

The Evanex Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization operating in Ghana's Western Region. We believe that access to quality healthcare, education, and economic opportunity is a fundamental right — not a privilege.

Through strategic partnerships with hospitals, local government units, and community leaders, we mobilize resources and expertise to deliver lasting change where it matters most.

Healthcare Infrastructure
Hospital renovations & equipment donations
Education & Skills
Scholarships, training & community learning
Community Welfare
Food relief, maternal care & social support

HEALTHCARE & PAEDIATRIC

Apinto Government Hospital Children’s Ward Renovation

The Evanex Foundation is spearheading a comprehensive renovation of the paediatric ward at Apinto Government Hospital — one of the most critical healthcare facilities serving the Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality. The project includes the full refurbishment of the ward, supply of 200+ medical devices, and improved sanitation for the 3,000+ children served annually.

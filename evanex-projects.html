Healthcare Paediatric

Apinto Government Hospital
Children's Ward Renovation

Tarkwa-Nsuaem Municipality, Western Region

The Evanex Foundation is spearheading a comprehensive renovation of the paediatric ward at Apinto Government Hospital one of the primary public health facilities serving Tarkwa-Nsuaem. The project covers structural rehabilitation of the ward environment, procurement and donation of over 200 medical devices including infant warmers, pulse oximeters, nebulisers, blood pressure monitors, and essential diagnostic tools. The initiative directly improves care conditions for the estimated 3,000+ children treated annually at this facility, ensuring that young patients in Ghana's Western Region receive the quality care they deserve regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Community Borehole & Clean Water Initiative

Installation of 3 boreholes providing clean, safe drinking water to over 1,200 households in Prestea, drastically reducing waterborne illness rates and relieving the burden on women and children who previously walked long distances to collect water.

A two-day health screening camp offering blood pressure, diabetes, malaria, and eye testing for 1,000+ residents of Huni Valley. The event brought together a team of volunteer doctors and nurses to deliver preventative care in a community with limited clinic access.

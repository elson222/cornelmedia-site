Our Story

Our Story,
Our Mission

The Evanex Foundation was founded to address the persistent healthcare and welfare gaps that affect communities across Ghana’s Western Region — one life, one community at a time.

Direction

Mission & Vision

Mission

What We Do

To empower communities in the Western Region through sustainable healthcare, education, and welfare programs that create lasting change.

Vision

Where We’re Headed

A Ghana where every citizen — regardless of location — has equal access to quality healthcare and opportunities for growth.

Meet the Founder

EG
Pharmacist Public Health Community Advocate Social Entrepreneur

Evans Ghartey

Founder & Executive Director

“We started the Evanex Foundation because we saw firsthand the suffering in our communities — hospitals without equipment, children without access to care. We decided that complaining wasn’t enough. We had to act.”

Evans Ghartey is a licensed pharmacist with a background in public health and community-based healthcare delivery. Drawing on years of working directly with underserved populations in Ghana’s Western Region, he founded the Evanex Foundation to bridge the critical gap between available resources and those who need them most. His work spans free health screening campaigns, medication access programs, and youth welfare initiatives that have collectively touched thousands of lives across the region.

Core Values

Integrity

Compassion

Impact
